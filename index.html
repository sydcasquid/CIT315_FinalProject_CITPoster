APPLIED COMPUTING

What is Applied Computing?

Applied Computing is a multidisciplinary field that integrates principles from computer science, information technology, and business to solve practical problems in various industries. Unlike theoretical computer science, which focuses on abstract concepts and algorithms, applied computing emphasizes the practical application of computing technologies to address real-world challenges. Professionals in this field work on developing software, managing networks, ensuring cybersecurity, and analyzing data to improve business processes and decision-making.

Professionals in this area work on building applications that meet user needs and business requirements.

Cybersecurity

About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. This field is crucial as cyber threats become more sophisticated, and organizations seek to safeguard their information and infrastructure.

Job Options:

JProtects systems and networks from cyber threats by implementing security measures and protocols.

Monitors and analyzes security systems to identify vulnerabilities and respond to incidents.

Provides expert advice on cybersecurity strategies and solutions to organizations.

Educates individuals or groups on cybersecurity principles and practices.

Networking

About Networking

Networking professionals design, implement, and manage communication systems that allow computers and devices to connect and share resources. This includes setting up local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and ensuring reliable and secure data transmission.

Job Options:

Plans and designs the layout of networks, ensuring efficient and secure communication.

Manages and maintains computer networks, ensuring they operate smoothly and securely.

Designs the structure and functionality of websites, focusing on scalability and performance.

Combines software development and IT operations to shorten the development lifecycle and provide continuous delivery

Data Management

About Data Management

Data management focuses on the efficient and secure storage, retrieval, and analysis of data. It involves creating and maintaining databases, ensuring data quality, and utilizing data analytics to support decision-making processes.

Job Options:

Manages and maintains databases, ensuring data integrity, security, and availability

Analyzes data to extract meaningful insights, supporting business decision-making processes.

Conducts research to develop new methods and technologies for data analysis and management.

Studies market trends and data to provide insights and forecasts for businesses.
